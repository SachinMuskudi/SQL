DQL Operations
--------------
RULES FOR JOINS CONCEPT:
-------------------------
  1. CREATE 2 TABLES NO OF ROWS AND NO OF COLUMNS SHOULD BE SAME 
  2. COLUMN DATA TYPES SHOULD MATCH 
  3. MAINTAIN SOME COMMON ROWS TO UNDERSTAND THE JOIN CONCEPT CLEARLLY [SINGLE INPUT MUST MATCH]


PURPOSE OF JOINS:
----------------
WORKING WITH MORE THAN ONE TABLE 


1. Projection Method 
2. Selection Method 
3. Joins 
   --------
   1. ANSI JOINS                2. NON-ANSI JOINS 
   
A) INNER JOINS                    A) EQUI-JOIN = 
                                  B) NON-EQUI JOINS
B) OUTER JOINS 
      a) left outer join 
      b) right outer join 
      c) full outer join 
C) CROSS JOIN 


INNER JOIN SYNTAX:
  SELECT *FROM <TN1> INNER JOIN <TN2> ON <TN1>.<CNAME> = <TN2>.<CNAME>
